Venn diagram color wheel: three overlapping HDR video circles (red, green, blue) on a black backdrop. Each circle stacks a video layer, a canvas layer, and a display-p3 overlay div, in that order. The canvas's alpha is driven per-pixel by an uploaded image's color channel (see below). The overlay div's alpha is a plain uniform black, controlled by its own slider, so you can dim an individual circle by fading it toward opaque black without changing the others. Green and blue circles use mix-blend-mode: screen so their light adds where they overlap red and each other, forming yellow, magenta, cyan, and white at the center, the same way overlapping stage lights mix.
