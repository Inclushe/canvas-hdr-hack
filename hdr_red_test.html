Each row compares the same hue three ways: an HDR-tagged video (BT.2020 primaries, PQ transfer, 10-bit), a plain SDR sRGB CSS color, and a display-p3 CSS color. On an HDR-capable display/browser the video swatch should look more saturated and brighter than the other two. On a non-HDR setup all three should look basically the same (display-p3 slightly more saturated than sRGB on wide-gamut screens).

red - HDR video
red - sRGB rgb(255,0,0)
red - display-p3 color(display-p3 1 0 0)
green - HDR video
green - sRGB rgb(0,255,0)
green - display-p3 color(display-p3 0 1 0)
blue - HDR video
blue - sRGB rgb(0,0,255)
blue - display-p3 color(display-p3 0 0 1)
white - HDR video
white - sRGB rgb(255,255,255)
white - display-p3 color(display-p3 1 1 1)
